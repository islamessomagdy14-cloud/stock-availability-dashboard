How to use this page

  1. Edit any numbers in the tables below — Tier Total, Sold, Port-out, or Pick. "Available" updates automatically as you type.
  2. Click "Generate updated file" — it copies the new file content to your clipboard (and shows it below just in case).
  3. Click "Open data.json on GitHub", click into the box, press Ctrl+A (Cmd+A on Mac) then Ctrl+V (Cmd+V) to paste over everything, then click the green Commit changes button on that page.
